## Brief

Ember had outgrown its original identity. The logo, colors, and type were chosen in a weekend hackathon three years earlier. As the company matured, the brand needed to mature with it.

<PlaceholderImage aspect="16/9" caption="Brand audit — inconsistencies across touchpoints" />

## Process

I audited every touchpoint — product, marketing site, pitch deck, social presence. The brand had drifted into inconsistency. I defined a tight system: one typeface family, a restrained color palette, and clear rules for illustration and photography.

<PlaceholderImage aspect="3/2" caption="Refined brand system — typography, colour, and illustration guidelines" />

## Outcome

- Brand recognition in target market increased 40% (survey-measured)
- Design team velocity improved — fewer decisions, clearer guardrails
- The refresh shipped across all touchpoints in 6 weeks

## Vision

Loop wanted to build fitness around accountability, not vanity metrics. The core loop: commit to a workout with friends, show up, celebrate together.

<PlaceholderImage aspect="16/9" caption="Group challenge flow — commit, show up, celebrate" />

## Key Features

Group challenges with flexible scheduling — no one gets penalized for life getting in the way. A minimal activity feed that highlights effort over output. No calorie counts, no body metrics.

<PlaceholderImage aspect="3/2" caption="Activity feed focused on effort and consistency" />

## Outcome

- 74% weekly active retention at 6 months
- Average group size grew organically from 3 to 7 members
- Acquired by a major fitness platform within 18 months

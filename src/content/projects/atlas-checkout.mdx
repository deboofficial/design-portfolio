## Problem

Atlas had a 7-step checkout flow with a 64% abandonment rate. Users were dropping off at the shipping and payment steps — too many fields, too much friction.

## Approach

I consolidated the flow into 3 steps: cart review, shipping + payment (combined), and confirmation. Address autofill, saved payment methods, and smart defaults eliminated most manual entry.

## Results

- Checkout abandonment dropped from 64% to 42%
- Conversion increased by 22%
- Average time to complete checkout reduced from 4.2 minutes to 1.8 minutes

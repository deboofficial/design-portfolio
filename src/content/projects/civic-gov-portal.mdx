## Challenge

A city government needed to move 40+ services online. The existing website was built for internal stakeholders, not residents. Literacy levels, language barriers, and device access varied widely.

<PlaceholderImage aspect="16/9" caption="Service catalogue — plain language navigation" />

## Approach

Plain language first. Every flow was written at a 6th-grade reading level. We supported 4 languages and designed for low-bandwidth connections. Progressive enhancement ensured core functionality worked without JavaScript.

<PlaceholderImage aspect="4/3" caption="Multi-language support across key service flows" />

## Impact

- Online service adoption increased from 12% to 67%
- Call center volume decreased by 35%
- Accessibility audit score: 98/100 (WCAG AAA)

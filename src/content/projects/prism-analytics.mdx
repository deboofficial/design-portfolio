## Problem

Small teams wanted analytics without the complexity of enterprise tools or the privacy concerns of big tech solutions. They needed answers to simple questions: what's working, what's not.

<PlaceholderImage aspect="16/9" caption="Single-screen dashboard — all key metrics at a glance" />

## Design Philosophy

One screen, no drill-downs needed for 90% of use cases. The dashboard answers five questions: how many visitors, where they come from, what they look at, how long they stay, and what device they use.

<PlaceholderImage aspect="4/3" caption="Five core metrics — visitors, sources, pages, duration, devices" />

## Results

- 10,000+ sites tracked within first year
- Average session time on the dashboard: 47 seconds (by design)
- Zero cookies, fully GDPR compliant
